Thank you for taking part in this test.

We're doing some testing on how easy it is for our users to input the members of a family, and their details, so we'd be grateful if you could imagine you are Dr. 08 08 born 08/08/0808. Your family members are:

  • Dr. 09 09 - born 09/09/0909
  • Dr. 10 10 - born 10/10/1010
  • Dr. 11 11 - born 11/11/1111

Use the About you panel to enter your details, and the About your family panel to enter your families details. When you're happy with all the details you've provided, use the buttons at the bottom with the titles of Add Details to tell us more about you and your family. One uses one table to enter the data (Add Details - TABLE); the other uses multiple tables (Add Details - TABLES). We don't have a preference for either as they both involve pretty much the same work, but we're interested in which is the easiest and most pleasant to use.
